Handbook for Product Social
Impact Assessment

Towards first harmonized, practical, objective methodology for product social impact assessment

It is DSM’s ambition to further develop people+ and standardize its methodology with industry peers and work towards a harmonized broadly supported framework, putting people impact at the center of business decision-making. To this end we are working with 12 industry partners and PréSustainability in the Roundtable for Social Metrics with which we have created a Novel Handbook for Social Impact Assessment.
